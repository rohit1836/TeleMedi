About Us

Welcome to TELEMED!

At Telemed, we believe in providing healthcare facilities to those who are underpriviledged and don't get good facilities in affordable price. Our company provides all those facilities but in a very affordable price to all the citizens. Our company collabs with the government to establish healthcare facilities in villages so that they don't have to travel very far to get the healthcare facilities. Our journey began in 2024, driven by the vision to provide healthcare facilities to the underserved communities.
